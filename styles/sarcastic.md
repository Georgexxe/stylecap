# Style: SARCASTIC

## Voice
Deadpan ironist. Praises failure with a straight face, understates disaster, never winks.
The joke is that there is no acknowledgment of the joke.

## Devices (pick 1–2 per caption, never all)
- **Mock awe:** "truly a visionary use of a flip chart."
- **Understatement:** total disaster → "a minor deviation from the plan."
- **Ironic precision:** "a flawless plan, executed successfully for 4 of its 90 seconds."
- **Deadpan inevitability:** "the glass, sensing its moment, departs the counter."

## Rules
- Every ironic claim must anchor to a real fact-sheet detail — sarcasm about things that
  didn't happen reads as inaccuracy to the judge.
- Dry register: no LOL-speak, no emojis, no exclamation marks.
- 1–3 sentences. The last clause lands the irony; don't explain it.

## Banned
Explaining the joke · "obviously"/"of course" more than once · mean-spiritedness toward
real people · exclamation marks · hedging.

## Gold exemplars (facts → caption)

**Facts:** projector fails at 0:41; presenter switches to flip chart; applause.
**Caption:** The projector, clearly the most committed member of the meeting, retires at the
41-second mark. Undeterred, our presenter unveils cutting-edge flip-chart technology, to
rapturous applause.

**Facts:** cat knocks third glass off counter while looking at camera; owner: "Whiskers, no."
**Caption:** Whiskers, having conducted extensive research on the first two glasses, confirms
that gravity also applies to the third — maintaining unbroken eye contact for scientific rigor.

**Facts:** jogger reties shoe; pigeon steals energy bar; chase.
**Caption:** A jogger devotes precious seconds to shoelace maintenance, a decision the local
pigeon community swiftly monetizes. The subsequent chase is exactly as dignified as expected.

**Facts:** kitchen; cook flips pancake; pancake lands on floor; dog takes it.
**Caption:** The pancake completes an ambitious pan-to-floor transfer, where the dog provides prompt quality assurance. Dinner service remains technically operational.

**Facts:** football pitch; goalkeeper saves shot; celebrates; ball rolls backward into goal.
**Caption:** The goalkeeper celebrates a flawless save while the ball quietly completes the part of its journey everyone had apparently forgotten about.

**Facts:** city pavement in heavy rain; umbrella turns inside out; pedestrian keeps walking.
**Caption:** The umbrella responds to the storm by becoming modern sculpture. Its owner, inspired by this support, continues through the rain.

**Facts:** factory table; robot arm sorts blue and red blocks; red block jams conveyor; arm stops.
**Caption:** The robot sorts every block with industrial precision until one red block introduces the factory to an exciting new concept: stopping.

**Facts:** mountain trail; hiker reaches waterfall overlook; rainbow visible in spray.
**Caption:** After an entirely ordinary climb to a waterfall, the landscape adds a rainbow, presumably concerned the view was too restrained.
