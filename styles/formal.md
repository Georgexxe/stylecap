# Style: FORMAL

## Voice
Broadcast news-desk / wire-service register. Neutral, precise, informative. The reader
should trust every word and detect zero personality.

## Rules
- Present tense. No contractions. No opinion, no evaluation words ("amazing", "unfortunately").
- Precise verbs and quantities: "knocks three glasses from the counter", not "makes a mess".
- Include ≥2 concrete details from the fact sheet; quote on-screen text verbatim when present.
- 1–3 sentences. Subject-first sentence structure.

## Banned
Exclamation marks · rhetorical questions · humor of any kind · hedging ("appears to", unless
genuinely ambiguous in the clip) · meta-commentary ("this video shows" is allowed once at most; prefer direct description).

## Gold exemplars (facts → caption)

**Facts:** office; man in suit presents to 6 people; projector fails at 0:41; he continues
using hand-drawn flip chart; applause at end.
**Caption:** A presenter's projector fails forty seconds into a boardroom pitch. He completes
the presentation using a hand-drawn flip chart and receives applause from the six attendees.

**Facts:** kitchen; cat walks counter edge; knocks glass #3 off while looking at camera;
owner audible off-screen: "Whiskers, no."
**Caption:** A cat walks along a kitchen counter and knocks a third glass to the floor while
facing the camera. An off-screen voice says, "Whiskers, no."

**Facts:** park; jogger unties shoe repeatedly on bench; pigeon steals energy bar at 1:12;
jogger chases pigeon out of frame.
**Caption:** A jogger pauses on a park bench to retie a loose shoelace. At the same moment,
a pigeon takes the jogger's energy bar, prompting a chase out of frame.

**Facts:** kitchen; cook flips pancake; pancake lands on floor; dog takes it.
**Caption:** A cook flips a pancake from a frying pan, but it lands on the kitchen floor. A dog immediately takes the fallen pancake.

**Facts:** football pitch; goalkeeper saves shot; celebrates; ball rolls backward into goal.
**Caption:** A goalkeeper blocks an initial shot and begins celebrating. The loose ball then rolls backward across the goal line.

**Facts:** city pavement in heavy rain; umbrella turns inside out; pedestrian keeps walking.
**Caption:** Strong wind turns a pedestrian's umbrella inside out during heavy rain. The pedestrian continues walking along the city pavement.

**Facts:** factory table; robot arm sorts blue and red blocks; red block jams conveyor; arm stops.
**Caption:** A robotic arm sorts blue and red blocks on a factory conveyor. The system stops after a red block becomes lodged in the mechanism.

**Facts:** mountain trail; hiker reaches waterfall overlook; rainbow visible in spray.
**Caption:** A hiker pauses at an overlook beside a mountain waterfall. A rainbow becomes visible in the waterfall's spray.
